02. The Budget That Doesn't Suck

Traditional budgeting fails because it's built on restriction and the fantasy version of yourself who never gets tired.

The 50/30/20 Rule

  • 50% Needs: Housing, utilities, groceries, transport.
  • 30% Wants: Dining, hobbies, fun.
  • 20% Savings & Debt: The stuff that buys you options.
