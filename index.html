CHAPTER 2: THE BUDGET THAT DOESN'T SUCK

Traditional budgeting is designed to fail because it's built on restriction and rigidity. We're building a system that acknowledges you're a human being, not a spreadsheet with feelings.

The 50/30/20 Rule

  • 50% on Needs: Housing, utilities, groceries, insurance.
  • 30% on Wants: Dining out, hobbies, the concert tickets.
  • 20% on Savings & Debt: Emergency fund, retirement, extra debt payments.

CHAPTER 3: DEBT ISN'T EVIL, BUT INTEREST IS

Interest is the killer. It's the difference between debt you can escape and debt that owns you. Every dollar you pay in interest is a subscription to being broke.

Snowball vs. Avalanche

Snowball: Kill the smallest debt first for the win. Avalanche: Kill the highest interest rate first to save money. We recommend the Snowball for the momentum.
